Maison de Parfum began as a deeply personal vision from our founder, Antonio Khazro. With a lifelong fascination for scent and its connection to memory and identity, Antonio set out to create more than just perfumes—he wanted to craft experiences. What started as a personal passion, experimenting with notes and compositions, soon grew into a commitment to building a house dedicated to true artistry in fragrance. Driven by the belief that scent is one of the most powerful forms of self-expression, Antonio spent years exploring traditional perfumery techniques, sourcing exceptional ingredients, and refining his craft. Maison de Parfum was born from this journey—rooted in creativity, precision, and a desire to offer something genuinely distinctive. At Maison de Parfum, our aim is to create fragrances that stand apart from the ordinary. We believe perfume should feel intimate and timeless, not mass-produced or fleeting. Each scent we develop is carefully designed to tell its own story—layered, refined, and unforgettable. We are committed to quality, authenticity, and innovation. From the selection of raw materials to the final presentation, every detail reflects our dedication to excellence. But beyond that, our mission is to inspire confidence and individuality—to help people express who they are without saying a word. Maison de Parfum is more than a perfume house. It is a celebration of identity, emotion, and the invisible art that lingers long after you’ve left the room.
