Maison de Parfum was founded by Antonio Khazro with a clear vision: to make exceptional fragrances more accessible while preserving the artistry and emotion behind every bottle. What began as a personal passion for fine perfumery and luxury scents soon developed into a curated destination for those who value elegance, individuality, and quality in fragrance. Antonio’s journey started with a deep appreciation for how perfume can define presence and evoke memory.

Over time, this passion evolved into Maison de Parfum—a space dedicated to carefully selecting and offering perfumes from renowned houses and niche creators alike. The goal has always been simple: to bring together a collection that reflects sophistication, character, and lasting impression.

At Maison de Parfum, we specialize in reselling authentic, high-quality fragrances that have been chosen with care and intention. Every perfume in our collection is selected not just for its name, but for its craftsmanship, uniqueness, and ability to resonate with different personalities and styles. Our aim is to bridge the world of luxury perfumery with those who seek it—offering a trusted place where customers can discover signature scents and timeless classics alike. We believe fragrance is personal, and finding the right one should feel effortless, inspiring, and meaningful.

Maison de Parfum is more than a retailer. It is a curated experience built around scent, identity, and the quiet confidence that comes with wearing something truly exceptional.
